WHAT WE DO ?

PT Arclus Indonesia Multisekuriti was established with the mission to provide comprehensive security solutions to businesses and individuals in Indonesia. The company was founded by a group of experienced security professionals who saw the need for a more sophisticated and specialized security service provider in the region.

The founders of Arclus had extensive experience in the security industry, having worked for several reputable security firms in Indonesia and other countries. They observed that most security companies in Indonesia provided only basic security services and lacked the expertise to handle complex security issues.

As a result, Arclus was founded to address this market void.The company's founders wanted to provide a one-stop-shop for all security needs, from security consultancy and risk assessment to manned guarding, electronic security systems, and security training.

Today, PT Arclus Indonesia Multisekuriti is a leading security company in Indonesia, with a team of highly trained security professionals and state-of-the-art security systems. The company has a proven track record of providing customized security solutions to businesses, organizations, and individuals, helping them mitigate security risks and protect their assets
