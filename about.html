ABOUT ARCULUS

Welcome to PT Arculus Indonesia Multisekuriti, a leading provider of innovative security solutions for the modern world. We are a team of passionate professionals who believe that technology can be used to make the world a safer and more secure place. Established in 2022, our company has quickly become one of the most trusted names in the industry.

At our core, we are a security company, but we offer much more than just traditional security services. We also provide various services for one of our premium services called Lynx360 is our subscription-based Geopolitical Analysis and Reporting product that provides valuable insights into security, politics, economy, and health and safety. Our team of expert analysts and researchers generates regular reports, including special briefs and customized reports, tailored to our client's specific needs. Our holistic approach to security means that we can provide our clients with comprehensive solutions that address all of their needs.

We understand that every business is unique, which is why we take the time to get to know our clients and their specific security concerns. We work closely with each client to develop customized security plans that are tailored to their needs and budget. Our goal is to provide our clients with peace of mind, knowing that their property, employees, and customers are all safe and secure.

One of the things that set us apart from other security companies is our use of technology. We believe that technology can be a powerful tool in the fight against crime, and we use the latest and most advanced technology to deliver the most effective solutions. From state-of-the-art surveillance systems to biometric access control systems, we use the latest and most advanced technology to keep our clients safe and secure.

Our team of highly trained professionals has years of experience in the security industry. We are dedicated to providing our clients with the highest level of service and support, and we are always looking for ways to improve and enhance our services. Whether you need traditional security services or more advanced solutions, we have the knowledge and expertise to meet your needs.

At our company, we believe that our success is directly tied to the success of our clients. That's why we are committed to providing our clients with the best possible service and support. We are always available to answer your questions and address your concerns, and we are constantly working to improve our services to better meet your needs.

If you are looking for a reliable, innovative, and technologically advanced security company, look no further than our company. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you protect your business, your employees, and your customers.
